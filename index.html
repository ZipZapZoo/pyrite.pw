FEATURES

Is It Really Fool's Gold?

PYE is designed to be a store of value cryptocurrency. Its future is largely dependant on community involvement and widespread adoption. Everyone is welcome to participate and contribute to making Pyrite more awesome!

PoW & PoS

Hybrid PoW/PoS consensus mechanism vastly reduces the chances of 51% attacks.

No Premine

There is no premine, no instamine, no cheating, no favoritism. Everyone has an equal chance to obtain PYE.

SHA-256Q

Our PoW mining algorithm is not compatible with the currently available SHA-256 ASICs. You can mine PYE using GPUs.

Open Source Community

Although the project was started by a single entity, the ultimate goal is to keep most of the decision-making unrestricted and democratic.

THE FUTURE

What Lies Ahead For Pyrite?

A roadmap!? There is none!

Pyrite is an experiment. It might even end up being a total failure; nobody knows. Ultimately, it is up to the community how far it can go and what road it will take. All ideas and even the smallest contributions are welcome. Fork it, play with it, explore!

While many blockchain projects come up with the most unimaginable goals and bizarre use cases, Pyrite, at its inception, promises absolutely nothing.
